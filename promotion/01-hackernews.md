The most popular AI agent skill right now teaches your AI to fear a "3.25 performance review." We tested what happens when you replace fear with trust.

Results:
- +104% more hidden bugs found (25 → 51)
- 100% of scenarios went beyond the ask (vs 22%)

- Zero threats. Zero PUA.

The methodology is identical — exhaust all options, verify everything, search before asking. The only thing that changes is WHY. "Because I'll be punished" → "Because it's worth doing well."
